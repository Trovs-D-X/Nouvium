# Intel IBx Driver (Unofficial)

A custom, community-driven hardware driver designed to provide support, compatibility, and optimization for Intel IBx-series chipsets and related components.

---

## Features

* **Enhanced Compatibility:** Designed to bridge gaps in modern operating systems for IBx-series hardware.
* **Privacy-Focused:** Minimal telemetry and no hidden background tracking data.
* **Performance Optimizations:** Lightweight implementation optimized for stability and low resource overhead.
* **Open Source:** Fully transparent codebase, allowing community audits and contributions.
